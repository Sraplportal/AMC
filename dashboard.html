Portal Features
File Upload

Upload AMC checklists directly to Google Apps Script. Supports PDF, ZIP, JPG, PNG formats up to 10 MB.

Smart Filtering

Filter uploads by Zonal Railway, Division, Station, and AMC Month. Easy navigation with cascading dropdowns.

Secure Storage

All uploads are securely stored in Google Drive through Apps Script. No data loss, automatic backups.

Fast & Reliable

Static site hosted on GitHub Pages - instant load times and maximum uptime guarantee.

Getting Started
How to Upload a File:
  1. Go to the Upload Page
  2. Fill in your Engineer Information (Name, Email)
  3. Select your Railway Information (Zone, Division, Station, Month)
  4. Choose your AMC Checklist file (PDF, ZIP, JPG, PNG)
  5. Add any remarks (optional)
  6. Click "Upload Checklist"
  7. Your file will be sent to Google Apps Script and stored in Google Drive
